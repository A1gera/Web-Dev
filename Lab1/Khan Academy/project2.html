Chicken in sweet-sour sauce with rice

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4 person
Ingredients Quantity
chicken 500g
rice 400g
ketchup 4 tablespoons
salt 1 teaspoon
soy sauce 4 tablespoons
sugar 2 tablespoons

Step 1: Rinse the rice with water and cook it.
Step 2: Cut the chicken and fry it 15 minutes.
Step 3: Mix the rest of ingredients and boil it with chicken.
Step 4: Serve and eat. Priyatnogo appetita)
